Welcome To Technical Panels

TP Technical Panels Co., Ltd. started in 2011 as a team of producing and selling composite panels. Laminated Panels for Structural, Interior, Protection and Ballistic applications. The last few years have seen TP growth to over 70 members of staff and diversify.

We now manufacture world class GPS tracked AS & G2 rated composite panel security vaults, custom produce armoured vehicles for security companies such as G4S, and provide GPS tracking for businesses requiring detailed reports on their vehicle fleet activity as well as our initial core business of producing composite panels.

A distinct advantage to doing business with TP Technical Panels Co. Ltd. lies in the fact that our products are eligible for preferential tariff schemes (0% import duty) for export into the following countries:

Algeria, Argentina, Australia, Bangladesh, Benin, Bolivia, Brazil, Brunei, Cambodia, Cameroon, Colombia, Cuba, DPR Korea, Ecuador, Egypt, Ghana, Guinea, Guyana, India, Indonesia, Iran, Iraq, Japan, Korea, Laos, Libya, Malaysia, Mexico, Morocco, Mozambique, Myanmar, Nicaragua, Nigeria, Pakistan, Peru, Phillippines, Republic Of Korea, Singapore, Sri Lanka, Sudan, Tanzania, Trinidad and Tobago, Tunisia, Venezuela, Vietnam, Zimbabwe.

Technical Panels has framework puschase agreement with Mercedes Benz Head Office, stuttgart germany which allows us to purchase commercial vehicles for armoring from Mercedes Benz Head Office directly for a discounted price.

We are “The Security Company” successfully delivering armored CIT vehicles with composite panels manufactured at our state-of- the-art factory. Our customers view TP as a partner of choice, our innovative products, intelligent solutions makes as a competent supplier in the security industry and strive for sustainable growth and development.

To be one of the key player in the Security Industry with continuous improvement and exceed customer satisfaction. Our goal is to provide superior quality turn-key solutions through timely customer interaction, custom design, manufacturing, and support of composite panel related solutions.

Our Solutions

Composit Panels

TP HPPE panels are the most advanced ballistic protection solutions currently available on the market, having the best weight to ballistic performance ratio. They are made with the lightest material available: Ultra High Molecular Weight Polyethylene Fiber (UHMW-PE), capable of delivering high protection levels at a lower weight compared to traditional steel or other high performance ballistic materials. The panels feature exceptionally high multi-hit capability, providing the user an outstanding degree of safety if hit by multiple projectiles or fragments.
